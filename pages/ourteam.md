# Meet the Project Team

If you are interested in joining the project and learning how you can contribute to trans linguistic research in North Carolina, please reach out to us by submitting a contact form.

**Archie Crowley (they/them)**
*Assistant Professor, Elon University*

Dr. Crowley's role on the project includes recruiting participants, conducting interviews, analyzing participant interviews, and engaging in community outreach. Crowley’s research is focused on language ideologies within transgender and nonbinary communities. See more about their work at [archiecrowley.com.](archiecrowley.com)

**Haley Kinsler (he/they/she)**
*PhD Student in Communications, Rhetoric, & Digital Media, North Carolina State University*

Haley's role includes recruiting participants, conducting interviews, analyzing participant interviews, and engaging in community outreach.

**Azul Bellot (she/her)**
*Undergraduate Research Assistant, Elon University*

Azul's role includes transcribing and analyzing participant interviews, and engaging in community outreach. Azul's research interests are focused on the the experiences of transgender Latina women in the South, language, and healthcare.
